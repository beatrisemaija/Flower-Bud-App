About us

Helping you show how important that special someone truly is with Flower Bud.

Beginings

Flower Bud was started by two buddies Rebecca and Milly in 2017. Both graduated in 2016 in London, Rebecca receiving a bachelor in event management and Milly in floristry. After years of friendship, both understood the value of flowers, whether it be a single flower or a bouquet.

Meaning

Flowers have a power to change the feeling of a room, the effect of an image and give a scent to associate a loving memory with. Flower Bud allows a flower enthusiast, professional or a visitor, to get what their heart desires.
